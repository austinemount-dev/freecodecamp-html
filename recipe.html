A recipe page

a recipe is a set of instuctions for preparing a particular dish

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs, milk and flour
  • a cat
  • cooking oil
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bowl of water
  • 1 table-spoon of salt

click here to view more ingredients

Instructions

  1. slaughter the cat and boil it for about 30 min
    2. the cat is first skinned before boiling
  2. cut the boiled cat into small pieces
  3. put 2ml of oil into a frying pan and put it on medium heat
  4. cut the onions and tomatoes int small pieces and add them to the frying pan
  5. stir to mix the content and add salt to it
  6. break the eggs then add to the mixture
  7. add the boiled meat pieces to the content in the frying pan
  8. add the milk and the flour
  9. add water to it then cover and allow it to boil for 30min
  10. your food is then ready to serve!

cautions!

some parts of a cat are not edible!

some of the parts are:

  1. the head
  2. tail
  3. skin
